## 2.0.0

### Major Changes

- 🎉 Initial release of `@asyncflowstate/angular` — Official Angular bindings for AsyncFlowState.
- `createFlow()` — BehaviorSubject/Observable-based flow with full lifecycle control.
- `createFlowSequence()` for orchestrating sequential workflows.
- `createFlowParallel()` for running flows in parallel with aggregate state.
- `createFlowList()` for managing multiple keyed flow instances.
- Compatible with Angular 17, 18, and 19.
- Full TypeScript support with generics.
- Works with `async` pipe and RxJS operators.
- Auto-revalidation on focus and reconnect.
